Overview

Jott is an 'opionated', static blog generator for Node.js hackers.

The opinions are as follows:

  • Navigations, tagging, headers, and categories are superflous for blogging.
  • Jade is superior to Markdown for people who are familiar with node.js
  • The sole purpose of a blog post is for people to read it.

Jott is available via the NPM.
